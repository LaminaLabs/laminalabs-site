Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 27, 2025

Lamina Labs is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile app OfflineGPT and our website at laminalabs.app. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us at laminalabsofficial@gmail.com. We typically respond within 2 business days.

Responsible Party

Lamina Labs

Data We Collect

OfflineGPT App

- No Personal Data: OfflineGPT operates entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data such as your name, email, or location.
- Chat Data: All chat history and conversations are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any server or cloud. You can delete this data at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.
- Speech Input: If you use the speech-to-text feature, your voice input is processed by your device's system (e.g., Google Speech Recognition on Android). We do not access, record, or store any audio data.
- Model Files: OfflineGPT downloads model files on first launch, including Mistral models (Q3_K_S, Q4_K_M, Q5_K_S, Q6_K), Gemma, and Rocket. These models are licensed as follows:
  - Mistral Models: Apache 2.0 License (details)
  - Gemma Model: Google Gemma Terms, no modification or retraining allowed (details)
  - Rocket Model: CC BY-SA 4.0 License (details)
These files are stored locally on your device and are not shared with us or any third party.

Website (laminalabs.app)

- No Tracking: We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking technologies on our website.
- Server Logs: Our hosting provider, Netlify, may log basic access data (e.g., IP address, timestamp) as part of their standard operations. This data is not accessed or processed by us. See Netlify's Privacy Policy for details.

In-App Purchases

- Google Play Billing: OfflineGPT offers in-app purchases (e.g., Pro Version) via Google Play Billing. All payment data is handled directly by Google Play. We do not collect, store, or process any payment information.
- Purchase Data: We only receive anonymized confirmation of successful purchases to unlock features in the app.

Internet Usage

- Offline Operation: After the initial download of model files, OfflineGPT operates fully offline. No internet connection is required for core functionality.
- Website: Visiting laminalabs.app may involve data transfers to Netlify for hosting purposes, as mentioned in Section 3.2.

Third-Party Services

- Google Play Services: Used for in-app purchases and speech-to-text (optional). See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
- Netlify: Hosting provider for laminalabs.app. See Netlify's Privacy Policy for details.

Legal Basis for Data Processing

Since OfflineGPT does not collect or process personal data, the processing is minimal and based on our legitimate interest to provide a functional offline application. Any data processed (e.g., local chat history) is managed entirely on your device.

Data Storage and Deletion

- Local Storage: All data (chat history, model files) is stored locally on your device and remains there until you manually delete it or uninstall the app.
- Deletion: You can delete all app data at any time by clearing the app's data through your device settings or uninstalling the app.

Data Security

- All data processed by OfflineGPT remains on your device and is protected by your device's operating system security measures.
- We do not have access to your data, and there is no cloud storage or server transmission involved.

Your Rights

Since OfflineGPT does not collect or process personal data, traditional data subject rights (e.g., access, deletion) do not apply. However:
- Local Data Control: You can delete all app data (e.g., chat history, model files) at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data via your device settings.
- Contact Us: If you have concerns, you can reach us at laminalabsofficial@gmail.com. We typically respond within 2 business days.

Children's Privacy

OfflineGPT is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at laminalabs.app/privacy.html. Significant changes will be communicated via the app or website, and we will update the "Last Updated" date accordingly.

Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at laminalabsofficial@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 2 business days.
