1. Data Collection

No personal data is collected or processed. All processing happens locally on your device. No analytics, tracking or cloud sync is used.

Purchases are handled via Google Play Billing. No payment data is stored or processed by us.

All app data (e.g. chat history, model files) stays on your device and can be deleted by the user at any time.
