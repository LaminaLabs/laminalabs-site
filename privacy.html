Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2025

Lamina Labs is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile app OfflineGPT and our website at laminalabs.app. If you have any questions, please contact us at laminalabsofficial@gmail.com. We typically respond within 2 business days.

Responsible Entity

Lamina Labs
Responsible Person: F. B. (natural person)
Contact: laminalabsofficial@gmail.com

Collected Data

OfflineGPT App

No Personal Data: OfflineGPT works completely offline. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data such as name, email, or location.

Chat Data: All chat history and conversations are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to servers or clouds. You can delete this data at any time by removing the app data or uninstalling the app.

Voice Input: When you use the speech-to-text feature, your voice input is processed by your device's system (e.g., Google Speech Recognition on Android). We do not have access to audio data and do not store it.

Model Files: OfflineGPT downloads model files on first launch, including Mistral models (Q3_K_S, Q4_K_M, Q5_K_S, Q6_K), Gemma, and Rocket. These models are licensed as follows:

  • Mistral Models: Apache 2.0 License (Details)
  • Gemma Model: Google Gemma Terms, no modification or retraining allowed (Details)
  • Rocket Model: CC BY-SA 4.0 License (Details)

These files are stored locally on your device and are not shared with us or third parties.

Website (laminalabs.app)

No Tracking: We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking technologies on our website.

Server Logs: Our hosting provider Netlify may log basic access data (e.g., IP address, timestamp) as part of their standard processes. We do not view or process this data. See Netlify Privacy Policy for details.

In-App Purchases

Google Play Billing: OfflineGPT offers in-app purchases (e.g., Pro version) through Google Play Billing. All payment data is processed directly by Google Play. We do not collect, store, or process any payment data.

Purchase Data: We only receive anonymized confirmations of successful purchases to unlock features in the app.

Internet Usage

Offline Operation: After the initial download of model files, OfflineGPT works completely offline. No internet connection is required for core functions.

Website: Visiting laminalabs.app may involve data transfers to Netlify for hosting purposes, as mentioned in section 3.2.

Third-Party Services

Google Play Services: Used for in-app purchases and speech-to-text (optional). See Google Privacy Policy for details.

Netlify: Hosting provider for laminalabs.app. See Netlify Privacy Policy for details.

Legal Basis for Data Processing

Since OfflineGPT does not collect or process personal data, the processing is minimal and based on our legitimate interest in providing a functional offline application. All processed data (e.g., local chat history) is managed exclusively on your device.

Data Storage and Deletion

Local Storage: All data (chat history, model files) is stored locally on your device and remains there until you manually delete it or uninstall the app.

Deletion: You can delete all app data at any time by removing the app data through device settings or uninstalling the app.

Data Security

All data processed by OfflineGPT remains on your device and is protected by your operating system's security measures.

We do not have access to your data, and there is no cloud storage or server transmission.

Your Rights

Since OfflineGPT does not collect or process personal data, traditional data subject rights (e.g., access, deletion) do not apply. However:

Control over local data: You can delete all app data (e.g., chat history, models) at any time by uninstalling the app or removing the app data through device settings.

Contact: For questions, you can reach us at laminalabsofficial@gmail.com. We typically respond within 2 business days.

Privacy for Children

OfflineGPT is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version is always available at laminalabs.app/privacy.html. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or website, and we will update the date under "Last updated" accordingly.

Contact

For questions or concerns about the privacy policy, please contact us at laminalabsofficial@gmail.com. We strive to respond within 2 business days.
